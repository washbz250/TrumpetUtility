Trumpet Utility

About

Trumpet Utility is being actively developed upon by Zach Washburn. This app is Open-source, and you may use my code as long as you include my name. If you wish to use the source, please email me as I do not have the source publicly available yet.

Developers

Support

If you have a problem with the app, please don't hesitate to contact admin@washbz250.x10.mx. You will usually receive a response within the day. If you email on a weekday, please expect an email at around 5-6 PM. If you email on a weekend, you can expect an email soon after you send it.

Purpose

This app was developed by a trumpet player for trumpet players. This app was created mainly due to the lack of free/open source trumpet reference type apps on the app stores.
